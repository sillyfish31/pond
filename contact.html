Hotel ZERMAMA Zermatt: 100m away from Sunnegga-Rothorn

This brand new renovated hotel in the centre of Zermatt is just a 5-minute walk from Zermatt Train Station and across the road of the valley station of the Sunnegga Express and 100m from the magnificent Sunnegga-Rothorn Mountrain ranges. It offers free WiFi. All rooms at here feature an LCD TV and a private bathroom. The Matterhorn Express Cable Car and the Gornergrat Monte Rosa train can be reached easily by the electric bus. The stop is located directly in front of Hotel Mama.
