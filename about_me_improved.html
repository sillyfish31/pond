About Me: Lucas Snow

Hello, I am Lucas Snow and welcome to Snow Trails Travel Agency.

I'm all about snowboarding, winter sports and exploring the coolest snow-covered destinations, and also, I am blind.

I started this website to offer more opportunity for other visually impaired tourists, such as myself, to be able to explore and visit these amazing snowy destinations, all for a fair price and low hassle.
